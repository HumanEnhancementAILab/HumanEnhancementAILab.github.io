Welcome to the Human Enhancement AI Lab (HEAL)!

We are academic researchers with a mission to explore how businesses, employees, and consumers can best harness AI for human thriving. By studying human-AI collaboration, through the lens of behavioral science and data analytics, our lab aims at maximizing opportunities for human enhancement and growth, while minimizing harm.

Research

The AI Research Hub investigates AI-human interaction as it relates to four broad research questions:

  1. Organizations: How to use AI optimally for organizational transformation?

  2. Consumers: How to empower consumer decisions, including financial decisions with AI?

  3. Society: How to act responsibly and ethically when using AI?

  4. Learning: How to fast-track human learning with AI?
